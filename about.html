About Wake & Waves

Charting the course for boating enthusiasts since 2020

Our Story

Wake & Waves was born from a simple observation: the boating community needed a trusted, comprehensive resource that spoke to everyone from dreamers looking at their first boat to seasoned captains with decades on the water.

Founded in 2020 by a group of passionate boaters, marine journalists, and industry veterans, we set out to create more than just another boating publication. We wanted to build a community where knowledge flows freely, experiences are shared openly, and the love of life on the water brings people together.

What started as a modest weekly email to a few hundred subscribers has grown into a thriving community of over 50,000 boating enthusiasts across six continents. Our readers range from weekend sailors to full-time liveaboards, from fishing boat owners to yacht club commodores, and from curious dreamers to experienced captains.

Every week, we deliver carefully curated content that informs, inspires, and empowers our readers to make the most of their time on the water. Whether you're researching your first boat purchase, planning your next cruise, or looking for maintenance advice, Wake & Waves is your trusted companion on the journey.

?? Our Mission

To empower boating enthusiasts at every level with the knowledge, inspiration, and community they need to confidently pursue their passion for life on the water.

? Our Values

  • Authenticity: Real experiences from real boaters, not marketing fluff
  • Accessibility: Making boating knowledge available to everyone
  • Community: Building connections that last beyond the marina
  • Safety: Prioritizing responsible and safe boating practices
  • Sustainability: Protecting our waters for future generations
