Frequently Asked Questions

How often will I receive emails?

Our main newsletter arrives every Sunday morning. Depending on your interests, you may occasionally receive special editions or urgent safety alerts.

Can I unsubscribe anytime?

Absolutely! Every email includes an unsubscribe link. One click and you're out. No questions asked, no hard feelings.

Is my email address safe?

We take privacy seriously. Your email is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties. We only use it to send you our newsletter.

What if I don't own a boat yet?

Perfect! Many of our subscribers are aspiring boat owners. We provide plenty of content to help you prepare for boat ownership and make informed decisions.

Can I change my preferences later?

Yes! Every newsletter includes a link to update your preferences. Change your interests, email frequency, or content types anytime.

Do you offer a mobile app?

Currently, we deliver content via email and our website. A mobile app is in development for 2025!
